# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Software engineers and tech professionals who value efficiency and high-end tools. They need a reliable way to automate job applications without losing quality in their representation. They expect a "Pro" tool that feels premium and reliable.

## Product Purpose
JobPilot AI bridge the gap between job searching and application submission through intelligent automation. It uses Gemini AI to ensure only high-quality matches are pursued, saving hundreds of hours of manual work.

## Brand Personality
Modern, Technological, Premium. An "Apple-Pro" aesthetic: refined, clean, and sophisticated. It uses subtle depth, precise typography, and smooth, purposeful transitions to create an experience that feels like state-of-the-art software.

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS templates, overused "startup" gradients, cluttered enterprise dashboards, and low-contrast "aesthetic" sites that sacrifice usability for style.

## Design Principles
- **Elevated Craft**: Every element follows strict alignment and spacing. It feels "built," not just coded.
- **Kinetic Clarity**: Transitions aren't just decorative; they guide the user through the pipeline steps (Scrape → Filter → Apply).
- **Intelligent Density**: Provides a lot of information (job details, AI reasoning) without feeling overwhelming, using smart hierarchy.
- **Digital Materiality**: Uses glassmorphism and subtle borders to create layers of depth, mimicking professional hardware interfaces.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG 2.1 AA. High contrast for critical status indicators. Support for reduced motion while maintaining the premium feel.
